I am a member of the [Roman Space Telescope](https://roman.gsfc.nasa.gov)'s [Galaxy Redshift Survey (GRS) Project Infrastructure Team (PIT)](https://roman-grs-pit.caltech.edu), leading the development of mock galaxy catalogues that are used as input for simulating grism images. These mock catalogues are made using the [Galacticus](https://github.com/galacticusorg/galacticus/wiki) semi-analytic model, run on the [UNIT](http://www.unitsims.org) simulations. These catalogues used a new calibration of the Galacticus model that you can read about [here](/publications/calibratingGalacticusPaper1).
